AUS 2025 Global DAY

Global Day is an annual cultural event at American University of Sharjah held under the patronage of Her Excellency Sheikha Bodour bint Sultan Al Qasimi, President of American University of Sharjah (AUS). One of the largest events on campus, Global Day showcases the cultural heritage of the many nationalities represented at AUS by turning the university campus into a festival of culture and color. Event highlights include pavilions representing various countries, traditional food, music and cultural performances.
